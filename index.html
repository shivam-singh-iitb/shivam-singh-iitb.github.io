Projects:

In B.Tech, I did the following Projects:

  • Development of Image Processing Technique to Reduce the Effect of Blurring under the guidance of Dr. Ashok Sharma (Senior Project Research Scientist, NCPRE) and Dr.Prabir Kanti Basu (Senior Project Executive Officer(Scientist), NCPRE).
    • This was the Summer Internship Project at NCPRE, IITB. Using edge spread function and laplacian filter, the raw photoluminescence images of Si-wafers and Solar cells were enhanced for proper visualization of defects and leading to quality measurement of the wafer or cell.
  • Implementation of Subtractive Clustering on Functional Magnetic Resonance Images under the guidance of Dr.Bandana Barman (Assistant Professor, Dept. of ECE, Kalyani Government Engineering College)
    • In this Final Year dissertation, a recent clustering algorithm, called Subtractive Clustering has been used to cluster regions of FMRI images, based on activities.
